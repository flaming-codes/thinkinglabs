HAI is a working term for the surface where a person deals with an agentic system as a whole, rather than with every underlying worker, tool, runtime, permission, and protocol endpoint separately.

In this frame, Claude on a user's machine, a managed ChatGPT workspace agent, or a similar personal/workplace orchestrator is not merely a chat interface. It is the human-agent interface: the proxy for intent, authority, context, approval, and taste at the border of a larger agent graph.
