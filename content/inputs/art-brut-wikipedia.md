Art Brut names a field whose value is partly tied to its refusal of polish, training, and official art-world legibility. The useful market intuition is not simply that these works are "raw", but that they sit at a cultural angle to professionalized aesthetic control.

That angle may become more valuable as generative image systems make high-fidelity finish cheap, abundant, and increasingly programmable.
